# Nimbus Oven Pro 12 Setup and Wi-Fi Pairing

## First-time setup
1. Plug in the oven and wait until the display shows the welcome screen.
2. Select your language and confirm the device region.
3. Open Settings, choose Network, and confirm that Wi-Fi is enabled.
4. Hold the Pair button for 5 seconds until the wireless icon starts blinking.
5. In the mobile app, choose Add Appliance and scan for the Oven Pro 12.

## If pairing fails
- Confirm that the customer is using a 2.4 GHz network because the Oven Pro 12 does not support 5 GHz onboarding.
- Ask the customer to move the router closer than 5 meters for the first pairing attempt.
- Restart the mobile app before repeating the scan.
- If the wireless icon remains solid, hold Pair and Start together for 10 seconds to reset only the network settings.

## Escalation guidance
Escalate the case if the oven still fails to pair after a network reset and a second onboarding attempt on a confirmed 2.4 GHz network.
