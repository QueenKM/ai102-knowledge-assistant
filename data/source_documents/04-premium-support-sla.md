# Premium Support SLA and Escalation Rules

## Response commitments
Premium support cases must receive first response within 30 minutes during business hours and within 2 hours after hours.

## Escalation triggers
- Safety incidents involving smoke, sparks, or overheating
- Repeat failures after the documented troubleshooting flow has been completed twice
- Customer reports of a failed replacement device

## Contact channel
Premium support can be reached at support@nimbushome.example or +1 555 014 7722.

## Case notes
Every escalation must include the product model, serial number, troubleshooting steps already completed, and the current customer impact.
