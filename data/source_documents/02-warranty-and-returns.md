# Nimbus Home Warranty and Returns Policy

## Return window
Customers can request a standard return within 14 calendar days of delivery when the product is unused and returned with all accessories.

## Warranty coverage
Nimbus Home offers a 24-month limited warranty for manufacturing defects on smart appliances purchased through approved channels.

## Evidence required
- Order number
- Product serial number
- Short problem description
- A photo or video if the issue is physical damage or an installation fault

## Non-returnable situations
Opened water filters, custom installation services, and products damaged by misuse are not eligible for a standard return.
