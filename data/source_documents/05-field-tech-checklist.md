# Field Technician Visit Checklist

## Before arrival
- Confirm the customer address and availability window.
- Review the serial number and previous support notes.
- Check whether replacement parts are already approved.

## On site
1. Photograph the device label before any repair work starts.
2. Record visible damage, leaks, or signs of overheating.
3. Test the device on a known-good outlet if power issues are reported.
4. Capture the final status in the case notes before leaving.

## Escalate immediately
Escalate immediately if there is evidence of smoke, burning smell, sparks, or a failed safety cutoff.
